What we do

Source

We source only the best selection of hardwood tailored to your own preferences. Luxury shades with a modern feel while resembling the natural structure of wood.

Installation

Whether you want it like this - or like that, we can do it all! Create a pattern that is like no other in the world for an exclusive feel.

Expertese

With over a decade of experience, we offer the highest quality expertese in the field. Whether it's inside or out, we have a solution for you to make your home or business look like no other!

Local

Even though we have had projects in UK, USA, and other parts of the world, we proudly support the local Irish businesses. We are proud to have worked with Irelands leading construction companies.

About Us

World Wide Flooring is Dublin's largest independent floor installations contractor with over 10 years of experience in the field, we offer only the highest quality service tailored uniquely for you. Our wood comes handpicked just the way you like it - Extra long or extra wide planks, selected from the finest parts of old-grown trees.
